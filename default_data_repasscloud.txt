# ABOUT
RePass Cloud is an Australian software engineering company based in Sydney, delivering enterprise software, cloud automation, and operational tooling for organisations that need secure, scalable, production-grade systems. Its work spans enterprise platform development, Microsoft 365 and identity automation, cloud infrastructure tooling, finance workflow systems, FinOps platforms, and PowerShell-based operational automation.

At the centre of its offering is Cinturon360, RePass Cloud’s actively developed enterprise SaaS platform. Built for organisations operating across layered environments, Cinturon360 provides a governed foundation for operational workflows, tenant-aware configuration, secure integrations, access control, and full audit visibility.

RePass Cloud’s broader focus is on engineering-led solutions for businesses with real operational complexity, delivering custom-built systems that prioritise control, accountability, and long-term maintainability.
